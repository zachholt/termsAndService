Privacy Policy for Tweetiful

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Tweetiful helps users capture and customize screenshots or media from public X/Twitter posts.

Information We Collect

We do not require account creation and we do not collect your name, password, or direct user profile data in our own backend.

The app processes:

  • Tweet URLs you paste or share into the app.
  • App settings saved locally on your device (for example capture preferences and premium status).
  • Advertising-related data via Google AdMob when ads are shown.

How We Use Information

  • To fetch tweet content for rendering and export.
  • To persist your in-app preferences between launches.
  • To provide ads in the free version and measure ad performance.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Tweetiful uses Google AdMob to display ads in the free version. The app requests non-personalized ads. AdMob may still collect device identifiers and other signals for ad delivery, fraud prevention, analytics, and measurement, subject to Google policies.

Learn more:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How AdMob uses data

Photos Access

If you choose to save captured media, Tweetiful requests photo library add access so exports can be saved to your Photos app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers such as Google AdMob for the purposes above.

Data Retention

Most app data is stored locally on your device. You can remove app data by uninstalling the app.

Children

Tweetiful is not directed to children under 13.
